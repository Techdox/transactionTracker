Welcome to the Transaction Tracker App

About the App

This app allows you to track your transactions and get a better understanding of your spending habits. You can add, edit, and delete transactions, and filter them by merchant, category, and reimbursability. You can also view summaries of your spending by category and see how much money you have left in your budget.

How to Use the App

To get started, click on the "Transactions" link in the navigation bar. From there, you can add a new transaction by clicking on the "Add Transaction" button. You can also filter and delete transactions using the form and the delete buttons, respectively. To view summaries of your spending, click on the "Summary" link in the navigation bar. This will show you how much you've spent in each category and how much you have left in your budget.
